Standing Against Injustice

\

On the day of Ashura in 680 CE, Imam Hussain ibn Ali resisted oppression despite overwhelming odds. His courage,\ compassion, and commitment to truth became a universal call to defend the vulnerable and uphold human dignity.\ Al Hussain Foundation draws direct inspiration from these timeless principles to combat poverty, illiteracy,\ and social marginalization in today\'92s world.
